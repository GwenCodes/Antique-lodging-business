Local Guide

Things to Do Nearby

  • Malalison Island Excursion

    Take a short boat ride to experience pristine white sand bars, rolling hills, and crystal-clear snorkeling spots.

  • Kawa Hot Bath in Tibiao

    Relax in a unique, oversized wood-fired cauldron filled with fresh spring water and local aromatic leaves.

  • Bugtong Bato Waterfalls

    Trek through beautiful rice terraces to discover a multi-tiered crystal-clear waterfall hidden in nature.

Culinary Experience

Flavors of Antique

  • Native Chicken Binakol

    A comforting local soup cooked with fresh coconut water, lemongrass, and ginger inside a bamboo tube.

  • Freshly Harvested Bandi

    Antique's signature native delicacy made of local peanuts fused together with sweet muscovado sugar caramel.

  • Coastal Seafood Feast

    Enjoy daily catches of grilled milkfish, rock lobsters, and local seaweed salad (Ar-arosep) sourced straight from the municipal waters.
