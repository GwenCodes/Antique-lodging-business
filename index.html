Explore Madrangca & Beyond

Get the most out of your getaway with nearby sights, activities, and local culinary delights.

🏖️ Madrangca Beach & Coastlines

Just a short walk or tricycle ride away. Relax on the local shores, watch pristine West Philippine Sea sunsets, and chat with friendly local fishermen.

🎭 Binirayan & Sinugba Festivals

Immerse yourself in Antique's premier celebrations! Experience rich theatrical street dances commemorating Malayan roots, alongside smoky, delicious community grilling fests.

🐟 Local Eats & Fresh Seafood

Indulge in budget-friendly open-air diners near the town plaza. Feast on fresh *sinugba* (grilled fish), native chicken dishes, and traditional Antiqueño delicacies.
